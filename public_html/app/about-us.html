Our Story

Bodhi Intellect Bodhi Intellect is a group of dynamic young professionals who excel in their own fields. It includes Psychologists, Yoga Trainers, Nutritionists, IT Experts, Communication and soft skill trainers and Mentors. We are training people in Leadership Development, Marketing and Sales, Team building proficiency, Communication and Interpersonal skills. We have well devised strategies, tools and course plan much different from those adopted in conventional workshops. Being a learning partner to every firm, we focus on training their people not only to achieve best results, but also to live an enlightened life so that the employees will be an asset to the organisation. Our aim is to develop multifaceted, goal oriented professionals who deliver their best in the work place. Our tailor made solutions for each business verticals will identify the talents in the work force, nurture it through fine crafted techniques and will point it to focus on organizational goals in a smart way. We also aim to provide workshops for children to improve their learning and interactive skills through a set of signature programs. Counselling services offered by us through webinars and seminars endeavour to address day to day issues faced by the kids and adults. We are using technology at its best to train mind and body. Our program is designed by incorporating well structured group of sessions to develop the in depth abilities, skills, interactions and activities. We also give the participants a fine set of values to imbibe in their lives so that they will perfectly deliver the best to themselves and those around them.
